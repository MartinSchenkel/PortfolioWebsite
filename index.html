  • Tetris AI

    • This was a solo project where I used reinforcement algorithms to teach my AI to learn how to play Tetris. I worked on this for about a week in total.
    • Link to a showcase video

  • Ampleman Engine

    • AmpleEngine is what two of my classmates and me called our own custom 2D Game Engine that we were supposed to make a MegaMan X2 Clone upon. The engine is a pure Entity-Component System. I was responsible for the Physics, Gameplay Mechanics and Enemy AI. I also build the levels using our custom level editor tool. The engine was created in C++ from scratch.
    • Link to a showcase video

  • RayTracer

    • In my first year of University we were tasked with creating a ray tracer from scratch in C++. It was not real-time needing about 10 seconds for every frame, but it had reflection and refraction on spheres and cuboids.

  • Hammer Fly

    • Hammer Fly is a personal project I worked on. I made it in Unity on my own. Some assets are from Kenney Assets and some are created by me. I developed it for Android and it is currently available on the Play Store
