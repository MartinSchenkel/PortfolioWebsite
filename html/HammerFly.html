What was the project?

Hammer Fly is a personal project I worked on. I made it in Unity on my own. Some assets are from Kenney Assets and some are created by me. I developed it for Android and it is currently available on the Google Play Store

The Trailer

What did I learn from the project?

This project was not the most challenging from a technical standpoint. However, it did teach me a lot about what it takes to publish an app. I also learned how to use Unitys Touch Input and how to serialize and deserialize things like score and settings to a device. I also researched Unity Ads and how to use them, but in the end decided not to put ads on the product.
